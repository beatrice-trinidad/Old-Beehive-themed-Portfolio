    Canvas is a main learning management system at Rutgers University, where 40,000+ university students use this educational platform to access their online classes.

    At the time, I was originally an assistant to organize the Rutgers Online Learning Conference. However, when the Center for Online & Hybrid Learning and Instructional Technologies (COHLIT) noticed my skill in graphic design, they asked me to work part-time in recreating the Canvas login page for Rutgers University.
