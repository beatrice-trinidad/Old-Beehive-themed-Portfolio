    In the summer of 2017, three major instructional technology departments within Rutgers University joined forces: Center for Online & Hybrid Learning and Instructional Technologies (COHLIT), the Office of Instructional and Research Technology (OIRT), and Instructional Technology & eLearning Solutions (ITeS).

    Ultimately, my supervisor and I were responsible to create a new website that combines all content from the three different department websites. However, we took the extra step to also with more modern web design.
