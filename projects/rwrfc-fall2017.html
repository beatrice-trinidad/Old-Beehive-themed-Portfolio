    Rutgers Women's Rugby Poster (Fall 2017)

    • Graphic Design
    • Tools Used:
    • Adobe Photoshop

    Since I am the Public Relations & Marketing chair of my university's women's rugby team, the board had asked me to create promotional materials for the fall season. I took inspiration from the posters of other Rutgers' Divison 1 sports teams, such as basketball and football.

    I made this relatively quickly since I was asked to make this last minute before the season started. Three different variations of the banner with the schedule, RWRFC (Rutgers Women's Rugby Football Club), and no text included.
