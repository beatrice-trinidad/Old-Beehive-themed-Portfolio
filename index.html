    Float like a butterfly, sting like a bea

    Ever since I was a young girl from Brooklyn, I’ve always been fascinated with art, people, and technology. Somehow, I found myself channeling these individual interests into unique outlets over the years:

    • Graphic design and music performance have been long-time hobbies that inspire my creativity
    • My passion of traveling the world with my friends or my rugby team only fuels my favorite hobby of meeting new people and experiencing new things
    • Pursuing a degree in Information Technology & Informatics from Rutgers University, the State University of New Jersey allows me to fulfill my curiosity for problem solving & computer programming

    Nowadays, I'm lucky to have found a way that intertwines all of these through my work experiences. Connecting people to technology in innovative ways is my thing - from teaching children about Java, social media marketing, to designing websites for people to use. Ultimately, my goal is to combine design and technology into a career that focuses on human-centered products and services.
